About

Leonard Delle Chiaie is the complete name of this self-taught artist based in Melbourne.
He was born in Melbourne, but his family moved to Argentina during his early years, and spent most of his life in this country. This, of course, influenced his preferences being Benito Quinquela Martin and Raquel Forner two of his firsts influences in the world of art.
But since he started traveling around the world he found new influences from different countries but is probably Australia the country which hosts most of his favourite artists and friends, such as David Taylor, Ross Patterson, Maxine Wade, It Hao Pheh, Joseph Zbukvic, and Lisa Wang.
The art of Leonard is mainly made in pencil and watercolour. He is, in fact, an active member of the Victorian Watercolour Society and participates in numerous exhibitions in Australia.
